Luis Mario Mora Hernández

3D Artist / Digital Art / Game Design

I'm Luis Mora (Doghost) 20 yrs, Mexican 3D artist. I'm excited about 3D and especially the development of video games.

I'm a passionate and disciplined young man who always looks for inspiration in other people, a very good observer and an excellent learner, I'm very analytical and meticulous, I always seek to apply what I've learned and improve from my mistakes. I like to say that I can see the ghosts of things because everything has a story to tell and I want to tell those stories through the objects.
Soon I will be an environment artist in a huge video game company and I will continue telling those stories that gamers like so much..

I have experience working with Blender, Maya, Substance Painter, Zbrush and Photoshop.
